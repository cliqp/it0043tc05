Alolan Ninetales

Type:

Ninetales Alolan Form is known as キュウコン (Kyūkon) in Japanese and was introduced in Generation VII.

This Ice/Fairy-type Pokémon is renowned for its beauty and grace, and it can produce ice crystals from its fur to freeze opponents in an instant.

Pikachu

Type:

Pikachu is known as ピカチュウ (Pikachū) in Japanese and
was introduced in Generation I.

This iconic Electric-type Pokémon stores electricity in its cheeks and releases powerful electric shocks when threatened.

Bulbasaur

Type:

Bulbasaur is known as フシギダネ (Fushigidane) in Japanese and was introduced in Generation I.

This Grass/Poison-type Pokémon has a plant bulb on its back that grows by soaking up the sun’s rays.

Charizard

Type:

Charizard is known as リザードン (Lizardon) in Japanese and was introduced in Generation I.

This Fire/Flying-type Pokémon is renowned for its fiery breath that can melt anything and its ability to fly at high altitudes.

Articuno

Type:

Articuno is known as フリーザー (Freezer) in Japanese and was introduced in Generation I.

This Ice/Flying-type Legendary Pokémon is celebrated for its majestic appearance and its ability to create and control ice, often causing snow to fall when it flies.

Butterfree

Type:

Butterfree is known as バタフリー (Batafurī) in Japanese and was introduced in Generation I.

This Bug/Flying-type Pokémon is admired for its beautifully patterned wings and its ability to scatter toxic spores to incapacitate its foes.
